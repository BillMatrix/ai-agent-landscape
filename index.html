Types of AI Agents

AI agents are categorized based on their capabilities and applications.

  • Task-Oriented Agents: Designed for specific tasks, like scheduling or data extraction.
  • Conversational Agents: Focus on natural language interaction, such as chatbots.
  • Autonomous Agents: Operate independently, making decisions without human intervention.
